# MJ VS
## Incumbent Failure Arbitrage Studio Simulator

Version 1.0

## Overview

MJ VS is a single-player venture studio simulation game inspired by the Incumbent Failure Arbitrage Studio model.

The player acts as a Studio Principal responsible for identifying incumbent failures, validating customer pain, building a wedge product, acquiring customers, and ultimately creating a successful PortCo.

Unlike startup simulators that begin with coding, MJ VS rewards disciplined validation, customer discovery, strategic partnerships, and revenue generation.

## Core Philosophy

1. Customer pain matters more than ideas.
2. Execution matters more than ideation.
3. Revenue matters more than activity.
4. Partnerships create leverage.
5. Ownership follows contribution.
