Simulation Purpose

This one-page simulator shows how contribution credit gets allocated over time as a startup advances through venture-studio milestones. Each event produces a scorecard entry, draws from one or more economic pools, and reduces the value remaining in those pools. Ownership is not guessed at the start — it is earned, documented, and reviewed as the venture becomes real.
